About

Kiroku is a Criminal Database which seeks to provide service to families, individuals and information seekers for the purpose of safety or investigation. Our customers are the public and our collaborators include government agencies such as the IndianKanoon, the National Registry of Sexual Offenders and Local Police Forces who provide us with database access obtained with the required permissions in order to ensure accuracy and crosschecking of legitimate information. Information relating to the convict, i.e., name, age, physical description, crime type, date of crime, location, arresting agency, rating, status are provided. A search facility is provided to the end users to search by name of convict, location and crime type. The information on such convicts are crowdsourced via forms which can be submitted by the general public with a copy of the FIR issued against the convict as proof, but the same would be made available on the website only after cross-checking with an official police database. A method to contact the admins and provide feedback and corrections is also provided in order to ensure no defamation or incorrect or inappropriate details have been posted.
